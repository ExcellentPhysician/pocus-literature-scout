Automated Echocardiographic Detection of Congenital Heart Disease Using Artificial Intelligence
👤 Lukyanenko P et al. 📅 Mar 2026 ↗ PubMed
The EchoFocus-CHD AI model was developed to automatically detect 12 critical and 8 noncritical congenital heart disease lesions from echocardiographic images, achieving expert-level diagnostic accuracy. The model's performance generalized across diverse global settings, demonstrating robustness beyond the training center. This could fundamentally democratize CHD diagnosis in resource-limited environments where expert echocardiographers are scarce or maldistributed.
Abstract
Delayed or missed diagnosis of congenital heart disease (CHD) contributes to excess pediatric mortality worldwide. Echocardiography is central to diagnosing and triaging CHD, yet expert interpretation remains a scarce and maldistributed global resource. We developed EchoFocus-CHD, an AI-enabled model for automated detection of 12 critical and 8 noncritical CHD lesions. The model demonstrated high sensitivity and specificity across validation cohorts, with performance approaching expert cardiologist level.
